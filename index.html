Welcome!

Brazilian Child Care of Marin is a California state-licensed provider, nestled in the Terra Linda neighborhood of San Rafael. We have been offering care and educational experiences for children for the last 15 years.

We provide care Monday through Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:30 PM, but may have special hours during holidays.

Our dedicated team is here to ensure each child receives the highest quality care and attention. If you would like to learn more about us, don't hesitate to get in touch with us.
